---
title: Adding a New Project
description: Create a new documentation project in Boris Sprickut.
---

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Dashboard">
    Log in and go to your Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    Press the "New Project" button on the main page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Details">
    Enter a name for your project and a description (optional).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click "Create". Your new documentation space appears in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Editing">
    Click your project name to add your first page or upload content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

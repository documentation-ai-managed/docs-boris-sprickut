## Getting Started

1. Log in to your Boris Sprickut account.
2. Click "New Project" on the dashboard.
3. Enter a project name and optional description.
4. Click "Create" to generate your space.

## Next Steps

- Use the sidebar to add pages.
- Organize content by dragging and dropping.

For more, visit the [Guides](/help-center/guides/add-project) section.

description: Fix access problems to your Boris Sprickut project.
---

## Symptoms
- See "Access Denied" message
- Unable to view or edit documentation

## Possible Causes
- Not logged in
- Missing project permissions

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login status">
    ````
    Ensure you are signed in at borissprickut.com/login
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review permissions">
    ````
    Ask the project owner to add your email in the project settings.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
